### The Optimization: MatMul-Free Architecture

**The Insight:** Traditional neural networks are inefficient because they rely on matrix multiplication (MatMul), which is:
- **Energy-intensive** (requires floating-point operations)
- **Hardware-dependent** (needs GPUs/TPUs)
- **Thermodynamically wasteful** (generates heat)

**The Solution:** Ternary weights (+1, 0, -1) with addition/subtraction only

**The Benefits:**
- **15 TOPS/W efficiency** (vs. 1-2 TOPS/W for traditional NNs)
- **Neuromorphic-compatible** (ready for Intel Loihi 2, Hala Point)
- **Edge-deployable** (runs on low-power devices)
- **Thermodynamically optimal** (minimal heat generation)
