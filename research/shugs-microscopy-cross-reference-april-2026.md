*   **Experiment**: Multi-Scale Cluster Interference Experiment (MUSCLE) using matter-wave interferometry.
*   **Particles**: Sodium clusters, approximately 8 nanometers in diameter, with a mass exceeding 170,000 atomic mass units (5,000 to 10,000 atoms).
*   **Method**: The clusters passed through three diffraction gratings formed by ultraviolet laser beams.
