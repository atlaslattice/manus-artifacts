# Comprehensive Report: Cutting-Edge Wellness Facilities in Texas with Oscar Health Network

This report details the findings of a thorough investigation into wellness facilities across Texas that offer a comprehensive suite of advanced therapeutic services and are in-network with Oscar Health insurance. The research prioritized facilities providing contrast therapy (hot/cold tubs), personalized TMS/MeRT, IV therapy, massage, and acupuncture.

While no single facility was found to offer all the requested services under one roof while also being in-network with Oscar, several facilities offer a significant portion of these services. The following sections provide detailed information on the most promising candidates, outlining their services, insurance information, and other key details.

## Key Findings Summary

The research indicates that facilities tend to specialize in either mental health services (TMS/MeRT) or physical wellness therapies (contrast therapy, IV therapy, massage, acupuncture). Finding a single location that excels in both and accepts Oscar insurance proved to be challenging.

*   **Most Comprehensive (out-of-network for most services):** **Alive and Well** in Dallas and Austin offers the widest range of services, including most of the user's requested therapies. However, their functional medicine and wellness services are generally not covered by insurance.
*   **Best for TMS/MeRT (in-network with Oscar):** **Anew Era TMS & Psychiatry** and **Heading Health** are strong contenders for TMS and other mental health services, and they are in-network with Oscar. However, they lack the physical wellness services requested.
*   **Best for Physical Wellness (some in-network with Oscar):** **Degree Wellness** and **IVTherapy2Go** offer many of the physical wellness services and accept some insurance, including Oscar for certain services. However, they do not offer TMS/MeRT.

## Conclusion and Recommendations

Finding a single facility in Texas that provides all the requested cutting-edge services and is in-network with Oscar Health is not currently feasible. The best approach would be to utilize a combination of facilities to access all the desired treatments.

For **TMS/MeRT and mental health services**, **Anew Era TMS & Psychiatry** or **Heading Health** are the recommended choices due to their specialization and in-network status with Oscar.

For **physical wellness services** such as **contrast therapy, IV therapy, massage, and acupuncture**, **Alive and Well** is the most comprehensive option, although it is largely out-of-network. For in-network IV therapy, **IVTherapy2Go** is a convenient mobile option.

It is recommended to contact each facility directly to confirm the most up-to-date insurance coverage and service availability.
