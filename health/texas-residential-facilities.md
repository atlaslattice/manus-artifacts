# Comprehensive Report: Texas Residential Treatment Facilities with Cutting-Edge Therapies

**Date:** March 3, 2026

## Introduction

This report presents findings from a comprehensive investigation into residential treatment facilities in Texas that offer a specific suite of cutting-edge wellness and therapeutic services. The primary goal was to identify residential (inpatient) programs that provide contrast therapy (hot/cold tubs), personalized Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) or Magnetic e-Resonance Therapy (MeRT), IV nutritional therapy, massage, and acupuncture, with a specific requirement for being in-network with Oscar Health insurance. The research process involved examining facility websites, third-party directories such as Psychology Today and Recovery.com, and insurance verification portals where accessible.

A significant challenge in this research was the intersection of three distinct requirements: a residential setting, a comprehensive and specific list of advanced therapies, and in-network status with a particular insurance provider (Oscar Health). While many outpatient clinics offer these services, and many residential centers offer some of them, finding a single facility that meets all criteria proved to be exceptionally difficult. Luxury residential facilities often operate on a private-pay basis, and those that do accept insurance may not be in-network with Oscar. 

This report details the most promising facilities that meet at least some of the user's requirements, providing a clear overview of their services, insurance status, and unique features to aid in decision-making.

## Detailed Facility Profiles

### 1. Solstice Southwest

**Solstice Southwest emerges as the strongest candidate**, offering the most comprehensive combination of the requested advanced therapies while also being listed as an in-network provider with Oscar Health.

*   **Location:** Irving, Texas
*   **Services Offered:**
    *   **Contrast Therapy:** Yes, the facility has a contrast therapy unit that can function as a cold plunge or a warm soaking tub [1].
    *   **TMS / MeRT:** Yes, they offer both MeRT (Magnetic e-Resonance Therapy) and TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) using a Magventure system [2].
    *   **IV Therapy:** Not explicitly mentioned.
    *   **Massage:** Not explicitly mentioned.
    *   **Acupuncture:** Yes, acupuncture is listed as a provided therapy [3].
*   **Oscar Health Insurance:** **Yes**, Solstice Southwest is listed as an in-network residential facility on both Recovery.com and ProjectKnow.com's Oscar provider lists [4] [5].
*   **Notable Differentiators:** Solstice Southwest provides integrative psychiatric care for both adults and adolescents, with a focus on treating complex mental health conditions. They offer a full continuum of care, from residential stabilization to outpatient programs. Their residential suites are designed for a small number of occupants and include kitchenettes, promoting a sense of autonomy.
*   **Website:** [https://solsticesw.com/](https://solsticesw.com/)
*   **Contact:** (214) 306-8447

### 4. Other Notable Facilities (Private Pay or Unconfirmed Insurance)

These facilities offer a significant number of the requested services but are either confirmed to be private pay or their in-network status with Oscar could not be definitively verified. They represent the highest tier of luxury and cutting-edge treatment in Texas.

**J. Flowers Health Institute** (Houston, TX)
This is a luxury, bespoke diagnostic and treatment center that offers nearly all requested services, including **MeRT/TMS, IV therapy, massage, acupuncture, and a hot tub/cold plunge**. However, they operate on a **private-pay, subscription-based model** and do not work with insurance companies [11].

**Enterhealth Ranch** (Van Alstyne, TX)
Enterhealth offers **MeRT/TMS, acupuncture, and massage** at their 43-acre residential ranch. They operate on a **private-pay basis** but may work with some insurance providers on an out-of-network basis [12].

**Driftwood Recovery** (Driftwood, TX)
Located on an 85-acre campus, Driftwood offers **massage, acupuncture, a hot tub, and an infrared sauna**. They do not offer TMS or IV therapy. Their insurance acceptance is unconfirmed and requires direct verification [13].

**Black Horse Health** (Houston, TX)
This facility offers **TMS, IV therapy, and acupuncture**. However, their residential program is listed as **private pay** [14].

## Conclusion and Recommendation

The search for a residential treatment facility in Texas that offers the full suite of specified cutting-edge therapies and is also in-network with Oscar Health reveals a significant gap in the market. The most technologically advanced and luxurious facilities, such as J. Flowers Health Institute and Enterhealth Ranch, operate primarily on a private-pay basis, placing them outside the user's insurance constraints.

Among the facilities that are in-network with Oscar Health, **Solstice Southwest in Irving, TX, stands out as the most comprehensive option.** It is the only facility confirmed to offer TMS/MeRT, acupuncture, and a form of contrast therapy (cold plunge/warm tub) while also being listed on Oscar provider networks. While it does not explicitly list IV therapy or massage, its combination of advanced brain therapies and holistic services makes it the closest match to the user's ideal criteria.

For a broader range of holistic services within the Oscar network, **Ripple Ranch Recovery** is a strong alternative, providing TMS, massage, and acupuncture in a restorative ranch setting. 

Ultimately, the user may need to prioritize which services are most critical. If TMS/MeRT and contrast therapy are the highest priorities, Solstice Southwest is the recommended choice. If a broader range of traditional holistic therapies (massage, acupuncture) is more important, Ripple Ranch is a viable alternative. Direct communication with these facilities is recommended to confirm service availability and the specifics of their Oscar Health coverage.
