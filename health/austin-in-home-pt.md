# In-Home Physical Therapy Options in Austin, TX with Oscar Insurance

This report provides a comprehensive overview of in-home physical therapy providers in Austin, Texas, who may meet your criteria of offering daily 1-on-1 sessions and accepting Oscar Health insurance. The research process involved a multi-faceted approach, including a review of the Oscar provider directory, an analysis of providers' websites, and a general search of web directories.

A key finding of this research is the distinction between "home health" physical therapy and "mobile/concierge" physical therapy. Insurance, including Oscar, typically covers home health services for patients who are certified as "homebound"—meaning they are unable to leave their home without considerable effort. For individuals who are not homebound but prefer the convenience of at-home care, many providers operate on a private-pay or out-of-network basis. This report will outline providers in both categories.

## Summary of Findings

Finding an in-home physical therapy provider in Austin that is explicitly in-network with Oscar and offers daily sessions for non-homebound patients is challenging. However, there is one highly promising option, **Luna**, that warrants immediate follow-up. Additionally, several out-of-network providers offer the requested services and can provide documentation for potential reimbursement from your insurance.
