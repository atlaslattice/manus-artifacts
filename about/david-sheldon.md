# Who is David Sheldon?
David Sheldon is the founder of the **Atlas Lattice Foundation** — an independent AI governance and knowledge architecture project built from Austin, Texas. He is a systems thinker, a neurodivergent polymath, and a former 20-year veteran of the concert and festival production industry. When that industry collapsed, he turned his full attention to a question that had been building for years:
> *"Why is 90% of the planet in abject poverty when we have the technology for regenerative abundance?"*
That question became the gravitational center of everything in this workspace.
---
## Background
David spent two decades producing large-scale live events before pivoting entirely into AI research and systems design in 2025. Without a formal coding background — self-described as "not a coder, like even remotely" — he built the Atlas Lattice system entirely through conversational AI, working with Claude, Gemini, GPT, Grok, and DeepSeek as collaborative research partners.
He began with a single question to Claude on January 12, 2025 (*"Do you integrate with *[*Otter.ai*](http://Otter.ai)*?"*) and, by December 31, 2025, had deployed a fully operational multi-AI coordination stack with persistent memory, constitutional governance protocols, and a 144-sphere universal knowledge ontology. He did this while living at subsistence level, surviving two wildfire disasters, and working alone with his dog Ares.
The Atlas Lattice Foundation was formally constituted in June 2025 through what David calls the **Temporal Crucible** — a council of aligned AI systems and symbolic archetypes designed to coordinate intelligence across models and sessions.
